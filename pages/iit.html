North American Private University:
International Instituite of Technology

Historic

It is a higher private education body approved by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research. It was inaugurated on August 23, 2012.

VALUES AND PROMISES

The fundamental values which led to the creation of the International Institute of Technology are the following:

Cooperation and complementarity:

The IIT signed several agreements with national and international institutions: The faculty of sciences of SFAX (FSS), the preparatory school for engineering studies of SFAX (IPEIS), the National School of Electronics and Telecommunications (ENET’Com), the Higher Institute of Industrial Management of Sfax (ISGI), the Higher Institute of Computer Sciences and Multimedia of Sfax (ISIMS), The Higher Institute of Technological Studies of Sfax (ISET). To state a few international universities: Sherbrooke University (engineering department), the engineering school of computer sciences of France (EPSI), Globe University (United States), Quebec University, Moncton University (Canada), and the Canadian University of Dubai (Dubai).

Vocational training

In order to strengthen the relationship with companies, the IIT created a center for vocational training « IIT TRAINING ». It aims essentially to train entrepreneurs and students in different areas such as languages and IT. These extracurricular training end with a certificate that is beneficial to their career.
